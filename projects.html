Disaster Recovery Network Simulation

This project simulates a real-world enterprise disaster-recovery network using Cisco Packet Tracer. The design includes redundant routers, dual ISP connections, and multiple data centers to ensure continuous connectivity even during failures. Using technologies such as HSRP, OSPF, and dynamic failover routing, the network automatically redirects traffic when a router, link, or data center goes offline. The final build demonstrates a fault-tolerant, highly available infrastructure similar to what real companies use to maintain critical operations during outages.
